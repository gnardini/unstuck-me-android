# Unstuck Me Android App

Unstuck Me is an app that helps you make decisions and where you can help others make their own.
Upload up to four different images and let people vote on the one they prefer or share the votation with your friends!

This app was created during the [Wackathon](https://www.facebook.com/wackathon) and got second place.
